Burger

Description

A burger starts with a seasoned beef patty, grilled to perfection.

It's placed on a toasted bun with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and condiments like ketchup or mayo. Simple and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Hamburger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese slices (e.g., cheddar, Swiss)
  • Pickles
  • Onions
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Season ground beef with salt and pepper; shape into patties.
  2. Grill patties until cooked to your preference.
  3. Toast hamburger buns.
  4. Assemble by placing patties on buns; add cheese, lettuce, tomato, pickles, onions.
  5. Top with ketchup, mayonnaise, and mustard.
  6. Serve immediately.
