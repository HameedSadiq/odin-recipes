Pizza

Description

Pizza starts with dough made from flour, yeast, salt, sugar, and water.

It's topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella, and various toppings, then baked until golden and bubbly. Simple and delicious.

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose flour
  • Active dry yeast
  • Warm water
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Dried oregano
  • Dried basil
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Olives
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm water with sugar.
  2. Mix with flour, salt, olive oil; knead dough, let it rise.
  3. Fry garlic in olive oil, add crushed tomatoes, salt, oregano, basil for sauce.
  4. Roll dough, spread sauce, add mozzarella and toppings.
  5. Bake at 475 Degree Fahrenheit until golden.
  6. Slice and serve
